Introduction

This is a presentation of a Shiny App developed for Courseras Developing Data products course. In this evaluation, the goal is to create an application that updates itself based on an input given by the user.

For this, I built an application that displays the top 10 best-selling games of each Nintendo home console as requested by the user.

Nintendo

Nintendo is one of the largest video game companies in the world. It is currently one of the three largest players in the console market, a position it has held for at least the last 30 years.

In that period, it has launched seven desktop consoles:

  • Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)
  • Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES)
  • Nintendo 64
  • Nintendo Gamecube
  • Wii
  • Wii U
  • Nintendo Switch
